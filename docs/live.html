Every frame is embedded with CLIP ViT-B/32 in your browser (WASM/CPU). To stay real-time on a CPU it samples a few frames per second and skips near-identical ones (a keyframe gate — the idea MidStream scales to production streams). The full design is in the ADRs; a stronger encoder (Qwen3-VL / ColPali) is the GPU upgrade.

CLIP via transformers.js · no server · nothing leaves your machine.
